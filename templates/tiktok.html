TikTok is a Chinese video-sharing platform that took over Gen Z since it's initial release in 2016. The social media platform is used for creating short-form videos from 15 seconds and up to a minute, in various genres such as; music, dance, comedy, and education.
The amount of followers and likes top users recieve in TikTok is in millions and billions. Taking into consideration that the US currently has ~328.2 Million people, having billions of followers means around the world acknowledgement.
The top songs on TikTok can be represent by the number of videos that were created with them. Those numbers are in millions as well, giving artists a tremendous exposure.
The scatter plot is in alignment with Tik Tok’s algorithm. Initially when a use creates an account and posts videos, they may receive a high volume of likes even if their follower count is low. But as a user’s presence grows, the number of likes they receive may not rise as their follow count rises. Essentially as followers increase after a certain threshold, number of likes don’t necessarily increase.
